# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis | Power BI Project
## 🚀 Project Overview

- This project delivers an end-to-end data analytics solution for Blinkit by transforming raw retail data into actionable business insights using Power BI.
- It focuses on analyzing sales performance, customer behavior, and outlet efficiency to support data-driven decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Problem
- Blinkit operates in a highly competitive quick-commerce market where speed, product availability, and customer satisfaction directly influence revenue.
- However, the business faces challenges in clearly understanding:
- Which product categories and attributes (e.g., fat content) are driving the most revenue
- How outlet characteristics (size, location, establishment year) impact sales performance
- Whether current operations are aligned with customer preferences and satisfaction levels
- Due to fragmented and raw transactional data, it becomes difficult to:
- Track key performance metrics in real time
- Identify underperforming outlets or categories
- Optimize inventory distribution and product strategy

## 💡 Objective
- The goal of this project is to build an interactive Power BI dashboard that transforms raw data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to:
- Monitor sales performance through key KPIs
- Identify high-performing products and outlets
- Improve customer satisfaction using rating analysis
- Support data-driven decisions for inventory and business growth

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) 
- 💰 Total Revenue Generated: ₹ 12,01,681+
- Achieved over ₹12 Lakhs in sales, indicating strong overall business performance and consistent demand across product categories.
- 📈 Average Transaction Value (ATV): ₹ 140.99
- Maintained a stable average order value, reflecting balanced pricing strategy and customer purchasing behavior.
- 📦 Product Diversity: 1,559 Items Sold
- Wide product range contributing to sales, showcasing effective inventory management and category coverage.
-⭐ Customer Satisfaction Score: 3.97 / 5
Close to a 4-star rating, indicating positive customer experience and product acceptance.

## 🔥 Key Business Insights
- 📊 Generated over ₹12+ Lakhs in total revenue, indicating strong sales performance
- 🛒 Maintained a stable average order value (~₹141) across transactions
- 📦 Wide product diversity with 1500+ items sold
- ⭐ Customer satisfaction is high with an average rating close to 4/5
- 🏬 Outlet characteristics like size and location significantly impact sales
- 🥗 Product attributes (like fat content) influence purchasing patterns

- Power BI – Interactive dashboard & visualization
- Excel / CSV – Data source
- Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation
